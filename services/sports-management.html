Personal Trainer

Prince Duwai-BSc

Sport Coaching - Personal Sprint, Strength and Conditioning Coach

Prince is an ex-athlete and Olympian who competed for Sierra Leone at the 1984 Olympics in Los Angeles, the 1987 World Athletic Championship Rome and numerous African and West African inter-continental Championships. Prince also competed at both National and International levels representing Sierra Leone in other sport such as handball, volleyball and basketball. Prince is fully qualified (BSc) Sports Coach.

Prince has been coaching athletes for over fifteen years and is also a teacher and mentor to the Local Development Coaches in the East region.

Specialist Coaching: Sprint-Acceleration / DecelerationExplosivenessAgility Balance & Co-ordination, Mobility, Stability and Flexibility Strength and Conditioning/Injury Prevention.a

Services provided:

  • Testing protocols (Identify strength & weakness)
  • Improve strength, speed, reaction & explosiveness (Football related)
  • Tempo runs (Aerobic & Anaerobic- Energy system)
  • Functional movement & acquisitional skills
  • Injury prevention (Implement a pre-hab/rehab programme)
  • Advice on nutrition & hydration (Food & supplement-depending on age)
  • Evaluation Testing Assessment Sport Psychology-Mentorship

Clients: Prince has worked with many young athletes and footballers, including Uche Ikpeazu (Watford FC-Striker), Rowan Ince (Brighton FC-Midfielder) and Maxwell Statham (Tottenham Centre half U17).
